How it works

✏️ Enter symptoms → 🤖 SVM predicts disease → 📋 Get full health plan

The model is trained on 132 symptoms across 41 diseases. Each prediction comes with medications, diet advice, precautions, and workout recommendations — a complete picture, not just a label.

Your well-being, our priority

Navigating healthcare can feel overwhelming. That's why every prediction is paired with actionable next steps — so you leave with clarity, not just a diagnosis name. From dietary advice to workout tips, every dimension of recovery is covered.
